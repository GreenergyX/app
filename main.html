About Us

This Waste Management App is designed to help users keep track of their recycling efforts and promote a sustainable environment. It is developed by Shaurya and Vihaan, two passionate individuals dedicated to conserving the planet's resources.

With this app, users can record the materials they have recycled, such as plastics, cardboard, cans, compost, and more. They can also upload images to showcase their recycling achievements.

Our aim is not limited to just our school but extends to all schools and communities. We believe that by implementing this app, we can collectively make a positive impact on waste management.

Tips & Tricks

Recycling is an important part of waste management, and it is essential to follow local recycling guidelines. Here are some tips and tricks:

  • Reduce and reuse items whenever possible.
  • Practice proper sorting of recyclables.
  • Rinse containers before recycling.
  • Flatten boxes to save space.
  • Dispose of electronic waste properly.
  • Compost food scraps and yard waste.
  • Buy recycled products made from recycled materials.
  • Educate others about the importance of recycling.

Remember, recycling is just one aspect of waste management. It is crucial to reduce consumption, practice reuse, and make mindful purchasing decisions to achieve a sustainable future.
